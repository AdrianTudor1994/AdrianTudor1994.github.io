Adrian Tudor

Growing up as a kid, living in Romania, I would watch American movies and dreamt of walking in the streets of "The Big Apple". Never for a second I thought I would ever step foot among the streets of New York City. But then I got adopted, and my fmaily and I moved to the U.S, one hour away from New York City. We visted pretty much every inch of the city. Here, I discovered myself and what I am good at. I guess I can say I am living the dream.
